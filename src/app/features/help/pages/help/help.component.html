Hi how can we help ?

The answers to all your questions about this site are below. You can refer to the contact section for more detailed answers.

For lost or worn material,the same or a new print is first obtained

If it cannot be found,another material of the same value is purchased and the worn out material is not given to the user

You can make a resevation for the material you want to borrow

If you do not return the material you borrowed by the deadline you will have to pay a fee of x amount for each day it is returned late

If you have any penalties,you can access them listed int the penalties section

No extension of time can be made for borrowed material

No more than 10% of a borrowed book or an article of the same issue of a magazine is reproduced.

You can make suggestions for materials that are not in the library system from the material suggestions section.
